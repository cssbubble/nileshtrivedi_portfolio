Interactive Physics Simulation In The Browser

This is interactive 2-d physics simulation in the browser written in a pure-Javascript. I learned here numerical integration methods, rigid body dynamics, constrained dynamics collision detection/response and the tradeoffs involved like accuracy vs. performance. This should be interesting to people developing games or other simulation apps for the web.
